About us

We bring together like-minded people who love creating things with their own hands.

Here in Pottery Club Campo de Ourique you can learn how to make simple yet beautiful and very useful cups, dishes, vases and basically anything you want.

Note! You can attend any event for free but tipping our beloved teachers would be extremely appreciated.
